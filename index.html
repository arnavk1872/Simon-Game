How to Play the simon game: Click on the Start button/Screen to start, one of the 4 colored blocks will blink. pressing it will in turn do the same to another/same block . You have to remember the pattern in which these blocks blinked and keep pressing the pattern as the levels progress . As the levels get higher it gets more difficult to remember . Try to go higher than level 20 I dare you ;)
